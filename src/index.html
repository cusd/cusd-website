What is CUSD?

Choosing to evolve from our predecessor, CU Solar Decathlon, our founder, Jeremy Blum, understood that “Renewable energy is not the only way to achieve a more sustainable future. It’s really only a small part of it.” We harness the diverse, interdisciplinary, and creative nature of our team to create immediate impact.

Our existence is predicated on the notion that student organizations are just organizations that happen to be comprised of students. We are capable people designing, building and discovering viable solutions, now. CUSD creates opportunities for students at Cornell to create holistic output through integrated design and experiential learning. CUSD is a lifestyle.
