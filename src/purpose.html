What We Believe

Practical Impact Trumps Environmental Activism

Our work goes beyond bringing awareness to people. We aim to make tangible, long-term impacts all over the world.

Design Must Be Sustainable

All of CUSD’s designs are economically, socially, and ecologically sustainable to the community that we’re serving.

Change Has To Be Technological and Strategic

We conduct cutting edge research to simultaneously develop technology and redesign how teamwork functions in our academic system.

We Can Set Precedents

CUSD demonstrates that a student organization is capable of working in professional partnerships and accomplishing terrifyingly ambitious projects.

Student Value Comes From Experiential Learning

CUSD students from any major can apply their education outside of the classroom, while developing valuable mentorships along the way.
